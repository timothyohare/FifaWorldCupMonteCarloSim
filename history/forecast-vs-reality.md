## Reading the numbers

- **Brier** — mean squared error of the probabilities; proper, so it cannot be gamed by hedging.
- **Skill vs base rate** — `1 − Brier(model)/Brier(base rate)`; 0 is no better than forecasting every team at the base rate, 1 is perfect.
- **AUC** — how often a randomly chosen achiever was priced above a randomly chosen non-achiever.
- **Permutation p** — the forecasts are held fixed and the k-of-n outcome labels reshuffled; this respects the contested-slots structure that a binomial test would ignore.
- **Caveats** — one tournament only (the out-of-sample evidence is `src/eval/calibrate.ts`); the daily captures share information, so read the per-day p-values as one signal, not many.
